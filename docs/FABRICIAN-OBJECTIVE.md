# Fabrician Objective

## Brand direction
Fabrician is a fashion brand for every age and everyone, but the launch focus is **kids clothing only**.

## Current launch focus
- ready-stock baby clothing
- 0–12 months old bodysuit-type items
- Bangladesh-first
- mobile-first installable web app

## Target buyers
- Bangladeshi parents
- middle and high-income mothers
- people buying kids fashion items
- family members buying gifts for babies
- future family matching dress buyers

## What makes Fabrician special
Fabrician should not feel like a regular ecommerce store.
It should feel like a **futuristic, soft, premium shopping world** for baby fashion.

## Core product split
### 1. Store
The real business right now.
Ready-stock baby items, normal purchase flow, simple conversion path.

### 2. Fabrician Studio
An AI-assisted trial / preview feature.
Users can describe what they want, upload references, and see a concept-style output.
This is not yet the main commercial pipeline.

## UX goal
The experience should feel:
- premium
- futuristic
- soft
- warm
- layered
- immersive
- memorable
- unlike a typical ecommerce template

## Visual direction
Use a “soft 3D illusion” rather than heavy 3D.
Good ingredients:
- floating layers
- soft light bloom
- curved surfaces
- premium spacing
- editorial product storytelling
- dreamy but clear interaction design

Avoid:
- generic grids everywhere
- stock-template hero sections
- overly childish design
- harsh sci-fi visuals
- visual clutter

## Commerce rules
- free delivery all over Bangladesh for now
- all major payment types should be structurally possible
- ready-stock products are fully purchasable
- generator outputs are **not** yet normal products
- custom-order advance payment rules are for a later phase

- easy to extend later
- much more distinctive than the current scaffold
